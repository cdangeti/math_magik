What is vedic math and Abacus?

Abacus

The abacus is a simple tool with beads on rods used to teach arithmetic. By moving the beads, children can quickly learn addition, subtraction, multiplication, and division, which improves mental math, focus, and memory. strengthens essential math skills while enhancing cognitive abilities like focus, memory, and problem-solving.


Vedic Math

Vedic math is a set of ancient Indian techniques for fast mental calculations. It uses specific methods to simplify tasks like multiplication and division, helping students solve math problems quickly and build confidence in advanced math.
